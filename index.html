About Malariaway:

Malariaway is a daily prescription medicine used to treat malaria (P. Falciparum). Created by a team of scientists in 2024, it's been scientifically proven to effectively treat malaria over a period of 10 years. Click here to learn more about the development of Malariaway.

Important Safety Information:

Before taking Malariaway, consult your doctor. Malariaway can cause serious side effects, including:

  • Headaches
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Diarrhea
  • Loss of appetite
  • Dizziness
  • Abdominal Pain
